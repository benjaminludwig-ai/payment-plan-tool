SOP Rules
📄
Only late invoices are eligible
Current or future-dated invoices cannot be included in a payment plan.
📅
Standard maximum is 3 months
Exception up to 6 months is possible but must be clearly justified.
👥
Over 6 months · tag Collections team
Do not escalate. Tag Collections team in the support case and wait for approval before offering any plan beyond 6 months.
💶
Over threshold · tag Collections team
Do not escalate. Tag Collections team in the support case and wait for approval before confirming any high-value plan.
⏱
3 days to accept · 7 days to first payment
Buyer has 3 calendar days to accept the proposal. Once accepted, the first payment must be made within 7 days.
📧
Confirm every plan by email
Include invoice list, total amount, instalment schedule, due dates and payment reference.
🚫
Never waive late fees
Always add all late fees to the total before calculating any instalment amounts.
🏦
No DCA invoices
Invoices already sent to a debt collection agency are excluded from payment plans.
⛔
Previous default means not eligible
If a buyer broke a prior payment plan, no new plan can be offered to them.
